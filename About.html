Our History:How We Started

In the month of April of the year 1987,the Lord spoke to our late Bishop Oscar Hosseya and revealed to him the name that he was to give to the ministry He had commissioned him to work in , which was:"Ministry Of Accomplishment Of The Great Commission". which actually carries the instruction he had been given of making disciples of Jesus in all nation of the earth through the gospel of Christ.


Our Mission:

Our ministry is committed to raising devoted disciples of Christ—men and women empowered to share the gospel, lead others to salvation, baptize new believers, and nurture them into mature disciples. Our mission follows a clear cycle: we reach out, win souls, equip and disciple new converts, and send them back into the field to do the same—spreading the message of Christ and making more disciples.


Our Belief:Statement Of Faith

The Full Gospel

We believe in the complete gospel and accept all 66 books of the Holy Bible as God's Word. It is our ultimate guide for faith and daily life, both for individuals and the Christian community.


The Holy Trinity

We believe in God the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit—three who are one, as revealed in Scripture (John 16:13-15, 1 Corinthians 8:6).


In addition to the Holy Trinity, we believe in salvation, the Church (Ecclesia), angels, and the manifestations of the Holy Spirit.
